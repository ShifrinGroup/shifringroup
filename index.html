About Shifrin Group

Shifrin Group is a Toronto-based management, investment, and advisory group focused on hospitality, real estate, and service-driven businesses.

The Group was established to combine operational expertise, disciplined evaluation, and structured growth strategy — bringing these together in opportunities where execution drives measurable performance. Its perspective spans service-based operating businesses, branded hospitality environments, and real estate-backed ventures.

Shifrin Group approaches each opportunity with a focus on alignment between ownership, operations, and long-term value creation. The Group is intentionally built to grow into a long-term operating and investment business, grounded in discipline and operational credibility.

Each engagement is selective. The Group participates meaningfully in the ventures it supports — as an investor, operator, or aligned partner.
